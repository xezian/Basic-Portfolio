About Me

What am I? I am a person on Earth learning the language that we use to produce content on the internet. I feel immensely fortunate to be alive at a time when technology is on the brink of many new and exciting developments. I am excited about learning to use computer code to accomplish real things in the world. I am committed to environmental and social justice, and to being a good reliable and honest person in general.

I like to have fun, get exercise, spend time with my family, with our animals, write and perform music, go out to live music, run, play scrabble, try new things, learn new things, cook, ride a skateboard, learn how to surf, eat, laugh, make jokes, watch some decent TV, do home improvement projects, sleep when tired, and just generally enjoy the experience of life.
